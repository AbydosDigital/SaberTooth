Class: ExpandingPolicy

ExpandingPolicy

Expanding Policy causes children to expand in the given direction filling the parent. Note: This Policy doesn't respect siblings - best for widgets with a s a sigle child.

new ExpandingPolicy(hostWidget [, orientation])
